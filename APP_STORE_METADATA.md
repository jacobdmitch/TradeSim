## App Description

### Short Description (170 chars for search results)
Learn cryptocurrency trading without risk! TradeSim uses live market data to simulate trades, test strategies, and practice portfolio management. No real money involved.

### Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Practice Crypto Trading Risk-Free**

TradeSim is the perfect educational tool for anyone interested in learning cryptocurrency trading and investment strategies. Using real-time market data from Coinbase, you can simulate trading decisions and see how they would perform — without risking a single dollar.

**Features:**

📊 **Live Market Data**
Track real cryptocurrency prices and trends from major markets. Watch how Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other top cryptocurrencies move throughout the day.

💼 **Portfolio Simulation**
Start with a virtual balance and make simulated trades. Track your total value, realized profits/losses, and see how your decisions impact your portfolio over time.

🔄 **Rotation Strategy**
Learn about momentum-based rotation strategies. The app analyzes markets and suggests when to enter, rotate, or exit positions based on trend indicators and technical analysis.

📈 **Technical Indicators**
Study moving averages (SMA), RSI (Relative Strength Index), and momentum signals. Adjust parameters to see how different strategies perform.

🔔 **Smart Notifications**
Get alerted when the rotation engine identifies potential opportunities. Stay informed without constantly monitoring the markets.

⚙️ **Customizable Settings**
Adjust technical indicator periods, rotation thresholds, and chart timeframes. Experiment with different strategies to find what works.

📱 **Beautiful Interface**
Clean, modern design with intuitive navigation. View portfolio performance, market data, rotation recommendations, activity history, and settings — all organized in easy-to-access tabs.

**Educational Disclaimer:**

TradeSim is a simulation tool for educational purposes only. It:
- Executes NO real trades
- Requires NO account or authentication  
- Connects to NO real funds or exchanges
- Is NOT financial advice

The "predicted edge" shown is a momentum/trend heuristic for educational purposes, not a forecast or recommendation. Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and real trading involves substantial risk of loss.

**Perfect For:**
- Students learning about cryptocurrency
- Aspiring traders practicing strategies
- Anyone curious about crypto markets
- Testing technical analysis approaches
- Understanding portfolio management

**Privacy:**
No account required. No personal data collected. All simulation data stays on your device.

Start learning about cryptocurrency trading safely with TradeSim!
